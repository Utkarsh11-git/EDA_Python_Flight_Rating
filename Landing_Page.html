Flight Satisfaction EDA Project

Welcome to the landing page of our Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project on flight satisfaction. In this project, we have conducted a comprehensive analysis of a dataset with various attributes related to air travel and passenger satisfaction.

Project Overview

Our dataset contains information on factors such as gender, age, type of travel, flight distance, in-flight services, departure delays, and more. We have explored this data to gain insights into passenger satisfaction and the factors that influence it.

Key components of our EDA project include:

  • Data Cleaning and Preprocessing
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
  • Visualizations and Insights
  • Statistical Analysis
  • Conclusions and Recommendations

We invite you to explore our findings and visualizations to better understand the dynamics of passenger satisfaction in the airline industry.

Dataset Information

The dataset we used for this project includes the following columns:

  • id
  • Gender
  • Customer Type
  • Age

The dataset was collected from airline passengers and covers various aspects of their travel experiences.
